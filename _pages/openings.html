PhD/MSc students

  1. I have multiple openings for PhD and MSc students.
  2. Department offers all graduate students about $4600/year TA salary.
  3. Entrance scholarship is available for students selected by the committee. (a few thousands of dollars)
  4. Students with A-class GPA receive Faculty of Science scholarship since their second year. (a few thousands of dollars)
  5. Research Assistant positions (supported by my research grants) are available for students and range from $7000-30000 per year (effective Sept 2023). The exact amount depends on the workload and funding of research projects.
  6. Outstanding students have opportunities to have "research" internships supported by MITACS and other sources. I do `not' suggest students take non-research internships, which may delay graduation.
  7. `Strong' students are also encouraged to apply AI4PH scholarship .
  8. UVic tuition is about $8000-9000 per year, which is much lower than most other universities in Canada.
